About this site

I tried to collect data from animal shelters and provide them for free to everyone in formats readable for human and computers. This project is not meant to harm animal shelters rights but truly help them. Provide another channel to popularize animal adoptions and support. Because it's an experiment, sometimes a page might be a total mess or might not work in your browser. Please accept my apologies.
